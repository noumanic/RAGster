# Account and billing

This article covers Helios cloud subscriptions, payment methods, and seat
management for team plans.

## Subscription tiers

We offer three tiers:

- **Personal** — 1 user, 5 devices, 30-day cloud history. $5/month.
- **Pro** — 3 users, 25 devices, 365-day cloud history, priority support.
  $19/month.
- **Team** — unlimited users (per-seat billing), unlimited devices, 5-year
  cloud history, SSO via SAML 2.0. $9/seat/month.

Annual billing saves 20% on every tier.

## Changing plans

You can upgrade at any time; the new tier prorates immediately. Downgrades take
effect at the end of the current billing cycle to avoid mid-cycle data loss
(e.g., if you downgrade from Pro to Personal, history older than 30 days
becomes unavailable on the cycle boundary).

## Payment failed — error B_PAY_011

B_PAY_011 means the card processor declined the renewal charge. Common reasons:

- Expired card. Update the card in **Account → Billing → Payment methods**.
- Insufficient funds. Add a backup payment method or pay the failed invoice.
- Address mismatch (AVS failure). Confirm your billing address matches the
  card-issuing bank's records.

We retry the charge three times over 5 days before suspending the account.
Suspended accounts retain data for 30 days; after that, data is deleted.

## Refunds

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on first-time subscription purchases.
Renewals are non-refundable, but you can downgrade or cancel at any time to
prevent the next charge.

## Switching from monthly to annual

In **Account → Billing → Subscription**, click **Switch to annual**. You will
be credited the unused portion of the monthly plan and charged the annual
amount on the same day.
