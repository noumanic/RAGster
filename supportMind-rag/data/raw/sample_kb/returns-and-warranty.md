# Returns, exchanges, and warranty

This article covers the full Helios returns and warranty policy. For RMA
(return merchandise authorization) requests, use the customer portal at
account.helios.example.

## Standard return window

You may return any unopened Helios product for a full refund within 30 days of
delivery. Opened products are eligible for a 90% refund within 14 days; we
deduct 10% to cover restocking and inspection.

## Items that cannot be returned

- Software licenses and digital goods.
- Custom-engraved devices.
- Items marked **final sale** at checkout.

## Warranty coverage

The Helios X4 carries a 2-year limited warranty against manufacturing defects.
The warranty covers:

- Battery degradation below 80% of rated capacity within 18 months.
- Charging-port failure not caused by physical damage.
- Wi-Fi radio failure confirmed by `helios-cli diag --net --hw`.

The warranty does NOT cover:

- Liquid damage, even on splash-resistant models.
- Damage from third-party power adapters not rated for USB-C PD 3.0.
- Cosmetic wear (scratches, scuffs).

## Filing a warranty claim

1. Sign in to the customer portal.
2. Open the device's record and click **File warranty claim**.
3. Upload photos of the issue and attach the diagnostic export from
   `helios-cli diag --export`.
4. We respond within 2 business days with an RMA number and prepaid shipping
   label.

Most claims are resolved within 7 business days of receiving the device.
