SAMPLE SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Acme Corp ("Client")
and Beta Consulting LLC ("Consultant") effective January 1, 2026.

1. TERM AND TERMINATION
This Agreement shall remain in effect for an initial period of twelve (12) months.
Either party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days' written notice
to the other party. Upon termination, the Client shall pay all undisputed fees
for services rendered through the termination date.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Client shall pay Consultant within thirty (30) days of receipt of each invoice.
Late payments shall accrue interest at a rate of 1.5% per month, or the maximum
rate permitted by law, whichever is lower.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party agrees to maintain in strict confidence all non-public information
disclosed by the other party. This obligation shall survive termination of this
Agreement for a period of five (5) years.

4. FORCE MAJEURE
Neither party shall be liable for any failure or delay in performance under this
Agreement that is due to fire, flood, earthquake, war, terrorism, pandemic,
governmental action, or other cause beyond such party's reasonable control,
provided that the affected party gives prompt written notice to the other party.

5. PENALTY CLAUSE
Should the Consultant fail to deliver agreed-upon milestones by the contractual
deadline, a penalty of 5% of the total fee per week of delay shall apply, capped
at 25% of the total contract value.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of
the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of law principles.

7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding
arbitration in Wilmington, Delaware, under the rules of the American Arbitration
Association.
